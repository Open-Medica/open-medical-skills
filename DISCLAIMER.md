# Disclaimer

## Research and Learning Tool

Open Medical Skills (OMS) is a curated directory of medical AI skills intended exclusively
for **research and learning purposes**.

### What OMS Is

- A community-curated catalog of medical AI agent skills
- A platform for discovering, sharing, and installing AI tools for medical research
- A resource reviewed by physicians for quality and safety
- An open-source project maintained by IntelMedica.ai

### What OMS Is NOT

- **Not** clinical decision support (CDS)
- **Not** FDA-cleared or certified medical software
- **Not** a substitute for professional medical judgment
- **Not** medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations

### Physician Review

Physician review on OMS is a **curation process** — it evaluates skills for quality,
safety awareness, and appropriate framing. It is **not** clinical certification or
regulatory approval.

### Intended Use

Skills listed on OMS are designed to assist with:

- Medical education and training
- Clinical research and literature review
- Healthcare workflow exploration
- AI capability prototyping in medical domains

### Liability

Users assume all responsibility for how they use skills obtained through OMS. No skill
on this platform should be used for direct patient care decisions without independent
clinical validation and appropriate regulatory compliance.
